About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from the Universidade de Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro (UTAD). There, I acquired the fundamental programming skills that have shaped my knowledge and that I will carry throughout my career. During this period, I made great friends that I want to keep for life. Then, due to my work and dedication, I was invited to join the HCILab at UTAD, where we developed a project entitled BartleZ, which successfully led to the publication of two scientific papers.

BartleZ (ACM Digital Library Article) BartleZ (SpringerLink Article)

I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Design and Multimedia at the Faculdade de Ciências e Tecnologia da Universidade de Coimbra (FCTUC), where I expand my skills at the intersection of design and technology. My passion lies in crafting meaningful, user-centered digital experiences that balance aesthetics, functionality, and innovation. I enjoy exploring emerging technologies and transforming complex ideas into clear, engaging, and intuitive web solutions. What drives me most is turning concepts into digital experiences that feel natural, purposeful, and enjoyable to use.
